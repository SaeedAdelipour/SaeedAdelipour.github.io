About

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Electrical Engineering Department at the Sharif University of Technology, Tehran, Iran. I currently work on distributed model predictive control via secure multi-party computation under the supervision of Prof. Mohammad Haeri at the Advanced Control Systems and Optimization Lab.
I received my B.Sc. in electrical engineering from Iran University of Science and Technology in 2011. I completed my M.Sc. and Ph.D. in control systems engineering at Sharif University of Technology in 2013 and 2020, respectively. From October 2017 until May 2018, I was a visiting researcher in Prof. Gabriele Pannocchia's Lab. at the University of Pisa, Italy.

My research interest includes Robust and Model Predictive Control, Decentralized and Distributed Control, and Secure Network Control Systems.
